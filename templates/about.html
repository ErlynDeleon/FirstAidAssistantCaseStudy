About First Aid Assistant

Your quick-access guide for essential first aid responses during emergencies.

Overview

The First Aid Assistant is a research-based expert system designed to suggest emergency actions based on incident descriptions such as choking, bleeding, and burns. It leverages a rule-based engine developed in Python to provide accurate, immediate advice to untrained individuals in critical moments.

Research and Development

This system was developed as part of a project for the Bachelor of Science in Computer Science, Information Systems at New Era University. The project was carried out by Richmond A. Baltazar and Erlyn Queen V. De Leon. The development aimed to explore the practical application of AI in public health and emergency preparedness.

Technology Stack
  • Backend: Python (Flask Framework)
  • Frontend: HTML, Bootstrap 5
  • Logic Layer: Custom rule-based inference engine
  • Design Goals: Responsive UI, quick input, reliable advice
